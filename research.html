Optical Optimization of Semitransparent Perovskite Devices Summer 2025
Research Intern · Fudan University · Prof. Yiqiang Zhan's Group

Worked on improving the optical performance of semitransparent perovskite devices for next-generation optoelectronics. Fabricated perovskite micro-arrays using laser micro-processing to quantify visible-light transmittance, and designed Ag/MoO3/Ag transparent electrodes to study chromaticity modulation. Used theoretical modelling to direct the experimental programme and presented results in weekly group meetings.

Validating the Higgs Boson Jun 2025
Imperial College London · Group of 4 · Python project

Simulated LHC collision data using NumPy and Matplotlib for a statistical analysis. Implemented Maximum Likelihood Estimation and chi-squared minimization for model fitting; reduced chi-squared from 2.83 to 1.13, validating presence of the Higgs at the simulated mass. Wrote up the analysis as a formal report.

The Kelvin Water Dropper Jun 2025
Imperial College London · Summer project · Group of 4

Investigated the charging mechanism of the Kelvin water dropper. Built a theoretical model relating air humidity to the charging rate and validated it against experimental data using regression and uncertainty propagation. Produced a 10-minute video presentation of the findings.
