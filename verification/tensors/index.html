Tensor index verification

Goal: every \tensor{base}{indices} call in the source must render with the upper and lower indices at distinct horizontal positions (μ above-left, ν below-right) — never stacked in the same column.

Approach: the build pipeline (build/tex_to_html.py:_rewrite_tensor) walks math regions and rewrites \tensor{R}{^μ_{νρσ}} to R^μ{}_{νρσ} (empty-group trick) before MathJax sees it. The empty {} separator is what makes MathJax kern the indices into separate columns.

The MIDDLE column below is live MathJax (same renderer as the deployed site). The RIGHT column is ground-truth pdfLaTeX with the actual tensor.sty package. They should look visually identical.
